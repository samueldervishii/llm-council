Usage Policy

Effective April 23, 2026

A short note on how Cortex is meant to be used, and the handful of things that will get an account shut down. The goal is a calm, useful workspace for everyone — not a rulebook.

What it is good for

Cortex is built to help with things like:

  • Thesis work, research, and academic writing
  • Learning new topics and asking for clearer explanations
  • Brainstorming, outlining, and organising ideas
  • Drafting and editing documents
  • Programming help and code review
  • General questions and everyday professional tasks

What is off-limits

Please do not use Cortex to:

  • Generate harmful content — material that promotes violence, harassment, discrimination, or anything illegal.
  • Deceive or manipulate — misinformation, impersonation, or fraud.
  • Infringe on someone's work — systematically reproducing copyrighted material or creating content that tramples on other people's rights.
  • Attack the service — probing for vulnerabilities, going after other users' data, or trying to get around safety measures.
  • Pass AI work off as your own where your school or employer requires you to disclose it. Cortex is a tool, not a loophole.

About the AI

Cortex is powered by Anthropic's Claude, and your conversations are also subject to Anthropic's Usage Policy.

Claude can be wrong, outdated, or over-confident. Treat it like a well-read assistant — a useful starting point, not a final authority on anything important.

Your data

  • Your conversations are tied to your account and only you can see them.
  • Shared sessions are visible only to people who have the share link.
  • You can export or delete your data from Settings whenever you like.
  • Uploaded files live alongside their session and are removed when you delete it.

Your account

You are on the hook for everything done under your account. Keep your login safe, do not pass it around, and know that accounts caught breaking this policy can be suspended.

Rate limits

There are soft technical limits in place to keep the service reliable and hard to abuse. They can shift as the product grows or when something unusual shows up in the traffic.

If this policy changes

Small updates happen occasionally. Continuing to use Cortex after one is posted means you are okay with the newer version.

Questions about this policy? Reach out on GitHub.
