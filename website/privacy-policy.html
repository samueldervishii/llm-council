Étude is a small research and writing workspace I built to help students and researchers get work done with Claude. This page is the plain-English version of what ends up on the server when you use it and what your options are for cleaning that up later.

What gets stored

When you sign up, Étude keeps the basics: an email, a password (saved as a salted hash, never the plain one), and whatever profile details you add later so responses feel a little closer to your voice.

Anything you type or upload during a conversation stays with that session so you can come back to it, pin it, or share it. If you would rather not keep it around, you can delete sessions by hand or turn on auto-delete so older ones fade out on a schedule.

Your browser keeps a little, too

Sign-in tokens and a couple of layout preferences — things like whether the sidebar is collapsed — live in your browser so the app does not forget you between page loads.

Where your data goes

Prompts and the relevant parts of your sessions are sent to Anthropic so Claude can answer them. That part is unavoidable for an AI product. Beyond that, Étude runs on standard cloud hosting and a database, and those providers only handle what they need to keep the service online. Nothing is sold, rented, or handed off to marketers.

If you turn on a share link, anyone with that link can read the shared session. It is the one moment where a conversation becomes visible outside your account, and you can revoke the link from the session menu whenever you want.

Cleaning up after yourself

  • Delete any session from the sidebar.
  • Set an auto-delete window in Settings so older sessions clear themselves out.
  • Export your chats as JSON or Markdown, or pull a finished document out as DOCX.
  • Delete the whole account, which clears the account record and what is tied to it on the hosted service.

Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, sessions use signed tokens, file uploads are checked before they land, and rate limits sit in front of the API to keep abuse in check. No online service is bulletproof though — if something is genuinely sensitive, think twice before handing it to any AI tool, Étude included.

Under 13s

Étude is aimed at students, researchers, and professionals. It is not designed for children under 13, and accounts should not be created on their behalf.

If this page changes

Small updates happen from time to time. When they do, the effective date at the top of this page changes with them.

Questions, concerns, or a mistake you spotted? The fastest way to reach me is GitHub.
