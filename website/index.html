Cortex is a clean, fast AI chat platform powered by Anthropic's Claude. Designed to help students write, research, and build their thesis — simply, securely, and affordably.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Real-time Streaming

Responses stream token-by-token as they're generated. No waiting for the full answer — start reading immediately.

File Uploads

Upload PDFs, DOCX, and text files directly into your conversation. Cortex extracts and understands the content.

Artifact Generation

Cortex detects when you need a document — thesis introductions, essays, reports — and renders them in styled cards with export to DOCX.

Secure by Design

JWT authentication, bcrypt passwords, account lockout, rate limiting, and encrypted file storage. Your data stays yours.

Personalized

Set your field of work, communication style, and preferences once. Cortex adapts to you across every conversation.

Export Anything

Export individual messages or entire sessions to DOCX. Download all your data as JSON or Markdown anytime.
