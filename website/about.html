Cortex is a small, focused workspace for students, researchers, and professionals who just want to get thinking and writing done. Less chrome, fewer tabs, a single quiet place to talk to one of the better AI models out there.

Why Cortex?

General-purpose AI tools weren't really built for long, research-heavy work. Cortex is. It wraps Anthropic's Claude in an interface that leans into drafting, source analysis, and academic writing instead of fighting it.

Built for academics

From a thesis outline to a literature review, Cortex knows the shape of the work. Drop in your research papers, get feedback that actually engages with the material, export polished DOCX drafts when you are ready to leave the chat.

Privacy first

Your chats are yours. Sensible storage, secure sign-in, and the ability to export or delete whatever you want from Settings. No tracking pixels, no data sold on, no surprises buried three pages into a policy.

Open source

The whole thing is open source. Read the code, send a pull request, or spin up your own instance. Transparency is not a marketing word here — it is how the project was built in the first place.
